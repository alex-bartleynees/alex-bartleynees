My Learning Plan

Bootcamp Learning Plan:

I would like to find work as a web or software developer. I have really been enjoying javascript and the logic and problem solving that it involves so I feel more drawn to the back-end programming rather than website design. I want to use the skills I learn to make a positive impact in the world.

I think my biggest strengths in Bootcamp will be my determination and patience. I am proud of myself during foundations for keeping at it even when I felt stuck and frustrated and for managing my time well. I feel like my determination means I will keep learning as much as possible and never give up because it feels too hard and that I will give Bootcamp everything I can. I think my limitations will involve self-confidence and not feeling good enough. I’m looking forward to pushing myself and I think I will grow at lot during Bootcamp.

I think my biggest non-technical challenge will be speaking in front of a group and giving presentations. I can be quite a quiet and shy person but I’m looking forward to challenging myself and rethinking my definitions of what I’m not good at.

I would like to develop more self-confidence and better communication skills. I would like to develop more leadership skills as I think this is an area I lack in, I can sometimes feel shy to put my ideas forward in a group and would like to challenge this.

My expectations from the Bootcamp team are that we’ll have a safe, fun learning environment but that we’ll also be pushed to work hard and develop ourselves and put our best self out there.

My expectations of myself on Bootcamp are that I’ll do my best to work as hard as possible and put a lot of effort in while keeping a positive attitude. I will do my best to embody the ideal of “joyful effort”. If I feel like I am struggling I will seek help from the facilitators and teachers and make sure to take care of myself by being kind to myself. I will do my best to work safely with others by being empathic and caring and trying to see things from other peoples points of view.

Foundations Learning Plan:

My long term goal is to find work as a web or software developer and to continue learning and growing as much as I can. My strengths are my determination, patience and passion for learning. When I want to learn something I always put a lot of effort in and I have a great ability to focus thanks to my meditation practice. My limitations are that I often want to try and do everything for myself and figure things out on my own which can make it hard for me to ask for help when I need it. I would like to develop my communication skills and confidence in myself and my abilities. I have a tendency to be quiet and can be shy to express my opinions so I would like to develop skills to be better able to express myself when needed. I will manage my workload by staying focused and breaking down what I want to achieve into manageable chunks. I will work productively and safely with others by being calm, respectful, kind and generous. I will take the time to listen to others and make an effort to communicate in a clear, effective manner. When things are building up and starting to get to me I will let others know how I am feeling and take time to meditate, do yoga and look after myself. I will commit to seeking help when needed through reaching out to facilitators and my peers. I will make sure to seek help when I need it and before I feel completely overwhelmed. I expect the facilitation team to be kind, generous and emphatic and available to listen and help. I will ensure I make time every morning for my daily meditation.
